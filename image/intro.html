3 INTRODUCTION Online Scheduling system - also referred to as Online Scheduling Software or Online booking applications or Online Scheduler. It is a Web based application that allows individuals to book appointments online through any web-connected device, such as a computer, laptop, smartphone or tablet. It is an automatic system of booking appointments and does not need the intervention of any medical staff.

4 The Benefits Saves Time Phone bookings are hectic and time consuming. An automated system saves valuable working time of medical staff.

5 Contd.. Saves Resources - The expenses borne for manual appointments and bookings are sidelined. - The reminders on automated systems reduce the incidences of no- show rates or missed appointments by approximately 50%, thus leading to savings.

6 Contd.. Book anytime, from anywhere Patients are not limited to any specific office-timings and bookings can be done round the clock at anytime, from anywhere.

7 Contd.. A more Convenient Approach Going online is an easy way of life. People take recourse to online transactions for a safer, convenient and smoother way of life. Making online appointments saves patients from undue hassles and enhances their experience.

8 Efficient Information Management An online system effectively registers all vital information of patients allowing you to forego manual entries resulting in chances of errors. Managing vital information online lets you manage them efficiently and more accurately.
